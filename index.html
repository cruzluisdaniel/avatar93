Bugs to patch

Not character flaws — predictable patterns baked into your specific chart combination. Naming them removes their power. Each comes with a concrete patch.

🚪

The escape hatch

Pisces' most persistent bug. When reality gets harsh the program defaults to dissolving — daydreaming, avoidance, over-idealism, losing yourself in someone else's world. The code will look for an exit. Notice when you're floating instead of swimming.

The patch

Name the escape when it happens. "I'm avoiding X right now." That one sentence interrupts the loop more reliably than willpower alone.

🔋

Buffer overflow from over-giving

High empathy + Virgo desire to fix + Capricorn need to prove usefulness = chronically over-giving and under-receiving. You'll run resource deficits for long stretches before the system crashes — usually through illness. The body keeps the score.

The patch

Build recovery proactively, not reactively. One full unscheduled day per week. Treat it like a meeting with your most important stakeholder — because it is.

🔊

The unreasonable inner critic

Virgo rising + Capricorn moon creates a brutal internal evaluator. You hold yourself to a standard you'd never apply to anyone else. The program mistakes self-criticism for quality control. It isn't — it's just drag on performance, creativity, and joy.

The patch

Ask: "Would I say this to someone I love?" If not, the critic isn't calibrated — it's noise. Redirect: "What would I tell a brilliant friend in this exact situation?"

🔒

The emotional vault

Capricorn moon makes vulnerability feel structurally unsafe. You convert feelings into tasks, or go quiet when you actually need connection. People close to you feel shut out without understanding why. This one doesn't self-correct — it requires a deliberate patch.

The patch

Practice selective visibility. Let people see the unpolished version in safe contexts. Start small. Stack experiences of the world not ending. The nervous system updates its model over time.

📊

Outcome attachment in disguise

You over-give because you need the outcome to validate the effort. You hold back from being seen because you can't control how it lands. You delay financial moves because outcomes feel tied to your worthiness. All of this is attachment running quietly in the background.

The patch

After completing anything — send the email, make the decision, have the conversation — consciously put it down. "That's done. I'm releasing the outcome." Practice daily.

⏱

Waiting until ready

Capricorn moon wants to show up with credentials. You wait until you're sorted, successful, or healed enough before stepping into leadership, visibility, or full commitment. The readiness condition never fully arrives — because it's powered by fear, not facts.

The patch

Ask: "What's the smallest version of this I could do today, imperfectly?" Do that. The doing creates readiness — not the other way around.
